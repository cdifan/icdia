CD-I News

On this page, you will find several issues of the CD-I News newsletter that was published by LINK Resources Corp. from November 1986, mainly to evangelize the coming CD-i platform. All issues are in Portable Document Format, you need Adobe Acrobat Reader to view or print them.

Please note that none of the addresses, phone numbers and other contact information listed in these issues is valid anymore. If you have any questions about these newsletters, you may contact me. Also, please contact me if you own additional copies of CD-I News either in digital form or in hard copy.

Thanks to Blazers for preserving and scanning these early documents. Full resolution versions of these documents can also be found on the Internet Archive.
