CD-i Pointing and Control Devices

Specifications, configuration, cabling and articles for CD-i player mice, trackballs, remotes, touchscreens, lightpen and other input devices.

Potential input devices to CD-i players include:

  • CD-i Mouse
  • CD-i Trackball
  • CD-i Rollerball
  • CD-i Touchscreen
  • MicroTouch Touchscreen
  • Graphics Tablet
  • Joystick
  • Remote
  • Bar Code Reader
  • CD-i Keyboard
  • Miscellaneous serial devices
    • Getting a particular device to work with a particular CD-i title may require some expertise, however.
