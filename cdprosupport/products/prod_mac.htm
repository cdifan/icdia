Authoring Products - Mac



Mac Adobe Photoshop Plug-In
Plug-in to Photoshop to enable exporting of pictures as CD-i types

Mac Animation Stack
A Hypercard application that converts PICS files and QuickTime movies to CD-i Run Length animation and DYUV movie formats. Requires Mac II or Quadra, 2.5 MB of RAM, Hypercard 2.2 or higher, QuickTime extension in system folder.

Mac Audio Stack
A Hypercard program for converting standard AIFF PCM and Sound Designer II audio files to the ADPCM format used in CD-i and CD-ROM/XA applications. Requires 2.5 MB RAM, Hypercard 2.2 or higher and 32 BIT QuickDraw installed.

Mac audio2mpeg
Reads AIFF or Sound Designer II audio files and converts them to MPEG-1 elementary audio streams.

Mac Balboa Runtime Mac
A "C" Application Programming Interface (API), containing libraries for still video effects, ADPCM audio playback and MPEG playback, and management and other tasks required in a CD-i title. Licensed for use by a single computer. Requires C Cross Compiler and CD-i Libraries.

Mac CD-i Authoring Board (PCI or NuBus)
A complete CD-i computer on a Nu-Bus or PCI card. Permits Macintosh machines to play Video CD and CD-i titles and also run CD-i authoring software, like MediaMogul.
Requires: Mac IIci or greater, open full length NUBUS slot or a half-length PCI slot, System 7 or higher OS, color NTSC RGB monitor, 8 MB RAM (16+ recommended), Apple CD300 or CD600 CD drive, 2.0 GB or larger hard drive.

Mac CD-i "C" Libraries
CD-i "C" libraries on Macintosh diskettes.

Mac deBabelizer
Automatic (batching) graphics processing and manipulation. Performs batch file conversions on over 30 Mac, PC, UNIX Amiga and other file formats, including CD-i. 8 MB or more system memory recommended.

Mac Master + Emulator + DiscTool
E2 Emulator & Master & Disctool for Mac

Mac Master/DiscTool
Macintosh version of Master. Master is used to process MPEG-1 real-time files, or to manufacture Video CD and CD-i disc image files, that are subsequently recorded to a CD. The disc type, layout and contents are described in a user created text file, called a "script". Successful use of Master requires advanced knowledge of various compact disc specifications.
DiscTool is used with the E2 Emulator computer to play disc images and/or record CD's to CDD 521/522/2000 Philips recorders.

Mac MediaMogul Migration Package
For sites wanting to migrate from 605-based development to Macintosh-based. Contents: Mac Authoring card, CD-R recording software, Mac Script2disc, Mac versions of their plug-ins.

Mac MediaMogul Studio Package
The MediaMogul Studio Package includes all the hardware and software necessary to author CD-i titles on a Macintosh. Contents: CD-i Authoring card, CD-R recorder & recording software,Ten blank CD-R discs, MediaMogul CD, Five CD-i Plug-ins of your choice, Graphics and Audio conversion software for CD-i, One year of fax/phone/email development support for entire package, Access to CD-i Professional Support Web site, Free subscription to Support Line newsletter, $500 training credit (training at a pmpro office only). Mac Photoshop Plug-in
An Adobe PhotoShop import/export plug-in for CD-i still image formats. Compatible with all PhotoShop versions 2.5 and later. Also compatible with Color It and deBabelizer!

Mac Pink
Multiplexes (combines) encoded elementary audio and video streams into a single MPEG stream ready for use in MPEG 1 applications. Single or multiple streams may be combined (up to 32 audio and 16 video). In addition to generic MPEG 1 format, Pink supports the special constraints of the CD-i and Video CD specifications.

Mac Script to Disc
Manufactures a CD-i disc-image from MediaMogul scripts, menus and assets. Requires System 7 or later OS.
