PC Master/DiscTool
PC/Windows version of Master. Master is used to process MPEG-1 real-time files, or to manufacture Video CD and CD-i disc image files, that are subsequently recorded to a CD. The disc type, layout and contents are described in a user created text file, called a "script". Successful use of Master requires advanced knowledge of various compact disc specifications. DiscTool is used with the E2 Emulator computer to play disc images and/or record CD's to CDD 521/522/2000 Philips recorders.

PC MediaMogul Migration Package
For sites wanting to migrate from 605-based development to PC/Windows-based. Contents: PC Authoring card, CD-R recording software, PC/Windows Script2disc, PC/Windows versions of their plug-ins.

PC MediaStockroom
Windows 3.x or 95 asset conversion tool for still pictures and ADPCM audio. Imports/Exports over 20 video and audio file formats. Full featured batch processing, database cataloging, including thumbnail pictures, are easy to use.

PC MPEG-1 Stream Verifier
Examines an MPEG elementary video, audio or multiplexed file for compliance to the ISO11172 MPEG-1 specification. The verifier generates very detailed reports for absolute errors or recommendation violations to the specification. Requires DOS 6.22 or later.

PC Photoshop Plug-in
An Adobe PhotoShop import/export plug-in for CD-i still image formats. Compatible with PhotoShop versions 2.5 and later.

PC pink
Multiplexes (combines) encoded elementary audio and video streams into a single MPEG stream ready for use in MPEG 1 applications. Single or multiple streams may be combined (up to 32 audio and 16 video). In addition to generic MPEG 1 format, Pink supports the special constraints of the CD-i and Video CD specifications.

PC script2disc
Manufactures a CD-i disc-image from MediaMogul scripts, menus and assets. Requires Windows 3.x or 95.

PC Video CD Toolkit for White Book 1.1

Track based tool for creating White Book 1.1 compliant Video CD's. Includes the Philips CD-i Application so that CD's play also on CD-i players.

The CD-i Disc Verifier is a tool used to analyze a CD-i disc to assure that the disc layout and structure are in conformance to CD-i Full Functional Specification, including the Full Motion extension. A report is prepared of all detected deviations from the standards.
