FontCatcher Articles

FontCatcher reads a CLUT 7 or Run Length 7 picture, extract glyphs, links individual gyphs to ASCII values, then manufactures a PRL font module. The font module can be used in MediaMogul, Balboa and many other CD-i title applications.
