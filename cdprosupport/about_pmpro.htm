About pmpro, inc.

pmpro's consumer support center is located in Urbandale, Iowa, a suburb of Des Moines. pmpro has another office in Washington, D.C., housing its sales, marketing, professional support, and engineering groups.

pmpro is a software developer, marketer, and support shop with product focus on Compact Disc Interactive (CD-i) and Video CD authoring tools and companion products. Our authoring tools are used worldwide to create mulitmedia training, educational, gaming and corporate presentations for playback on CD-i and VCD players. We design tools for MacIntosh, PC Windows, Sun UNIX and Microware OS-9 operating systems. pmpro's direct customers are professional video and multimedia title producers, corporate training and marketing groups, and school media departments.

pmpro has marketing, sales, engineering, and support groups to fully service the professional developer.

The Web site you are currently visiting is the effort of the Professional Support Group, who offer phone, fax and email support to developers. This Web site was designed to provide timely. accurate and comprehensive technical information, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week to anyone in the world. For many developers it also provides a cost effective communications medium to pmpro Support.
