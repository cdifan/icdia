Training Schedules and Information


pmpro Support does not conduct regularly scheduled training classes. Instead, we schedule them as requested, according to staff availability. Our staff can train you on general CD-i, MediaMogul, MediaMogul Plug-ins, title design and production, Video CD 2.0 authoring, and general MPEG encoding issues related to VCD or CD-i. We cannot advise or train you regarding "C" programming, either generally nor specific to CD-i.


Training conducted in the Urbandale office is $375 per day, regardless of content. You provide your own travel and lodging.

Training conducted at your business site is $625 per day, regardless of content. You are also responsible for reasonable travel and lodging expenses of the instructor.


For more details, please contact support cdprosupport@icdia.org or call 1-800-234-5484.
