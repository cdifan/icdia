VCD 2.0 Toolkit MPEG Attributes

PC and Mac VCD 2.0 Toolkits have specific requirments for MPEG assets, which mostly mirror those in the VCD 2.0 standard (White Book), and are mostly the same as each other:

Asset Type VCD Toolkit Identifier
  • Native still formats (to be converted to MPEG stills)
  • Elementary MPEG stills*
 STILL
Elementary Audio Streams (64,96,192kbit/s mono or 128,192,224,384kbit/s stereo)* ELMPA
Elementary Video Streams (1.1519kbit/s or less)* ELMPV
Multiplexed streams:
  • 128,192,224,384kbit stereo audio-only
  • 64,96,192kbit mono audio-only
  • 1.1519kbit/s or less video with non-224kbit audio
  • 1.1519kbit/s or less video-only
 MPGAV
Trackable multiplexed streams:
Contains 224kbit/s stereo audio and 1.1519kbit/s or less video.		 VCDAV

*Elementary streams may be imported into the Mac VCD 2.0 Toolkit only. The PC VCD 2.0 Toolkit requires audio-only/video-only streams and MPEG stills to be multiplexed prior to being imported.
