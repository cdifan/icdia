Avoiding Black Flashes between consecutive MPEG clips


If MPEG clips are played consecutively in a script, black flashes may occur.

  • The problem only occurs when playing the MPEG clip to the end, without an EP file loaded, and then immediately playing another clip.

  • Loading an EP file(any EP file) using the EPLOAD command, even if you do not intend to offer any scanning features, cures the problem.
