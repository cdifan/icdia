DiscTool Mac/PC/Sun

DiscTool is an emulation/recording utility for use with a Mac, PC or Sun host computer and the OptImage model E1 or E2 Emulator computer. The Emulator computer is MANDATORY for use with this product. Emulation also requires the 605 or 605T Development CD-i player.
