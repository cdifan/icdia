Balboa 4.x Articles/Code

Balboa is a low level CD-i authoring tool based on the C programming language. It consists of a number of CD-i specific runtime routines which offer a powerful way of accessing all of CD-i's potential. It is available for the Sun, PC, & Mac platforms.


Recording VCD and CD-i Disc Images in Windows 95/NT with Gear or WinOnCD.
