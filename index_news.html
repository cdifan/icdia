Welcome to the New International CD-i Association!

This website is dedicated to the Compact Disc Interactive system. It provides you with lots of technical background information, downloads, overviews and other resources on CD-i. More about the ICDIA...
What's New (Recent changes...)

January 14, 2001
  • Added a fantastic article by Alan Cawson and Leslie Haddon of the University of Sussex and Ian Miles of the University of Manchester, giving an in-depth overview of the conception and marketing of CD-i over the years. A lot of interviews held by the authors resulted in a unique inside view of CD-i's history starting in the late 70s. It should be read by anyone with the slightest interest in multimedia in general and CD-i in particular. It can be found in the Articles section.

    January 9, 2001
  • Added the latest version of the CD-i Filesystem Driver for Windows, the tool that allows you to read the contents of a CD-i disc and view MPEG sequences from CD-i titles on a PC, to the PC/Windows download section under Disc Tools. It has many new features and bug fixes, and now comes with the program sources! Also updated the article about the driver.
