Historical advertisements and publications

This brochure was jointly published by Philips and American Interactive Media (the Philips/PolyGram joint venture that was set up to develop CD-i consumer software prior to the launch of the hardware). It was intented to give a general overview of CD-i's functionality in a consumer area. The brochure was presented to visitors of multimedia fairs and business partners at around 1989. The CD-i unit (including the remote control) that is displayed is an artist's impression of a player, it was never actually produced. The first consumer CD-i player, the CDI 910, appeared on the market in the US in October 1991.
