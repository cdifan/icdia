Accesories for Philips Portable CD-i Players

On this page, you will find a complete overview of all accesories that were made for Philips' portable CD-i players. Please take note of which accesories are suitable for which players.

22ER9142
Digital Video docking station for CDI 310, CDI 350 and CDI 360

This docking station allows the CDI 310, CDI 350 and CDI 360 to be extended with a 22ER9141 Digital Video cartridge.
See manual for 22ER9142.

22ER9158
Rechargeable battery for CDI 370

Rechargeable battery, especially designed for the CDI 370.

22ER9213
A/V In cable for CDI 370

Allows the CDI 370 to display video from an external CVBS source on its LCD display.

22ER9270
Carrying Case for CDI 310, CDI 350 and CDI 360

Plastic carrying case, especially designed for the CDI 310, CDI 350 and CDI 360. It provides room for players with the Digital Video docking station attached.

22ER9271
Carrying Case for CDI 370

Plastic carrying case, especially designed for the CDI 370.

Others
For the CDI 310, CDI 350 and CDI 360, the following items were also available: a rechargeable battery pack, an external battery charger, a DC-DC adapter for battery use (as 22ER9153, see manual for 22ER9153), a car sigaret-light adapter, an RGB to Scart cable (as 22ER9206), an RGB In connection cable (as SBC 1138) and a dedicated RF modulator kit (as SBC 1137 + SBC 5411).
