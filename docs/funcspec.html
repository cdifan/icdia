CD-i Full Functional Specification (Green Book)

On this page, you will find documents related to the official specification of the CD-i system, a.k.a. the Green Book. Most of them are in Portable Document Format, you need Adobe Acrobat Reader to view or print them.


  • Compact Disc Interactive Full Functional Specification

    • Philips Consumer Electronics B.V. - May 1994
    This document is the official specification of the CD-i system. It was originally licensed under strict non-disclosure agreements but has been freely available online since at least August 2011.
    Download cdi_may94_r2.pdf (4420 KB - PDF)

  • Changes from March, 1993 to May, 1994

    • Philips Consumer Electronics B.V. - May 1994
    This document lists the changes between the March, 1993 and the May, 1994 version of the "CD-I Full Functional Specification", adding the DC_SetAR function and FMV-related CSD attributes and clarifying and correcting some minor issues.
    Download cdi_may94_chg.pdf (109 KB - PDF)

  • Changes from September, 1990 to March, 1993

    • Philips Consumer Electronics B.V. - March 1993
    This document lists the changes between the September, 1990 and the March, 1993 version of the "CD-I Full Functional Specification", adding chapter IX on FMV and clarifying and correcting lots of minor issues according to the implementation of CD-RTOS 1.1.
    Download cdi_mar93_chg.pdf (228 KB - PDF)

  • Recommendation for the implementation of full motion

    • This document recommends the combination of the FMV extension with a 1MB RAM extension. This combination is present in all released FMV cartridges and players with FMV capability.
