CD-i Technical Specifications / Chip Datasheets

On this page, you will find datasheets for a number of chips used in CD-i systems or devices. Most of them are in Portable Document Format, you need Adobe Acrobat Reader to view or print them.

These Chip Datasheets are mainly of interest to emulator writers.


  • SCC66470 VSC

    • Philips - September 1991
    This document describes in full detail the SCC66470 Video and System Controller (VSC) two of which are used in early CD-i players in combination with a Video System Decoder (VSD). It delivers some information about CD-i's video features, including technical aspects such as memory handling.
    Download scc66470.pdf (2122 KB - PDF)

  • MCD212 VDSC

    • Motorola, Inc. - 1995
    This document describes in full detail the MCD212 Video Decoder and System Controller (VDSC) that is used in later CD-i players. It delivers extensive information about CD-i's video features, including technical aspects such as memory handling.
    Download mcd212rev0.pdf (873 KB - PDF)

  • MCD221 CIAP Technical Summary

    • Motorola, Inc. - February 1995
    This documents summarizes the MCD221 CD-Interface and Audio Processor (CIAP) that is used in later CD-i players. It contains som information about CD-i's audio features and the way the system communicates with the CD drive.
    Download mcd221tsrev0.pdf (286 KB - PDF)

  • MCD251 VMPEG Technical Summary

    • Motorola, Inc. - January 1995
    This document summarizes the MCD251 MPEG1 Full Motion Video Decoder (VMPEG) that is used in some Digital Video cartridges as well as CD-i players with integrated FMV capability. It delivers some information about CD-i's MPEG1 video features.
    Download mcd251ts.pdf (1139 KB - PDF)

  • MCD270 IMPEG Technical Summary

    • Motorola, Inc. - February 1995
    This document summarizes the MCD270 IMPEG Integrated Video and Audio Decoder that is used in some Digital Video cartridges as well as CD-i players with integrated FMV capability. It delivers some information about CD-i's MPEG audio/video features.
    Download mcd270ts.pdf (376 KB - PDF)

  • MC68341/MC68341V

    • Motorola, Inc. - November 1995
    This archive contains the Product Information and Manuals for the MC68341 Integrated CD-i Engine. The MC68341 consists of an 68020 CPU, a two channel DMA controller, two serial channels, a timer and a serial interface. It is used in the CDI 370 portable, among others.
    Download mc68341.zip (1795 KB - 4x PDF in ZIP)

  • DSP56001

    • Motorola, Inc. - May 1998
    This archive contains the technical data for the DSP56001 Digital Signal Processor that is used in some Digital Video cartridges.
    Download dsp56001.zip (689 KB - 4x PDF in ZIP)

  • M48T08

    • STMicroelectronics
    These documents describes in full detail the M48T08 Timekeeper RAM. This IC contains SRAM that is used in CD-i players as 8KB NV-RAM, as well as a real-time, battery backed clock.
    Download m48t08.pdf (149 KB - PDF - May 1999)
    Download m48t08-m48t18-m48t08y.pdf (495 KB - PDF - June 2011)

  • DS1643

    • Dallas / Maxim
    This document describes a compatible replacement for the M48T08 Timekeeper RAM.
    Download ds1643-ds1643p.pdf (204 KB - PDF)

  • DS1216

    • Dallas / Maxim
    These documents describes in full detail the DS1216 SmartWatch RAM socket. The socket is used in CD-i with an SRAM chip as 32KB NV-RAM, as well as a real-time, battery backed clock.
    Download ds1216-ds1216h.pdf (660 KB - PDF)

  • DS1244

    • Dallas / Maxim
    This document describes a compatible replacement for the DS1216 SmartWatch RAM socket.
    Download ds1244-ds1244p.pdf (639 KB - PDF)

  • CD-i Base Case Video Board

    • Motorola Ltd. - July 1994
    This application note describes the design of a CD-i Base Case Video (BCV) board which forms part of the Motorola CD-i Development System (MCDS).
    Download bcv.zip (2732 KB - 1x PDF in ZIP)
