CD-i video formats - DYUV


Best for natural images, where color and brightness gradients are subtle.
Good for images containing anti-aliased text or reduced contrast objects.
16.7 million colors.
No CLUT (Color Lookup Table, otherwise known as a palette).
Full screen size of 384 x 240 -or- 384 x 280 pixels.

Trivia
Compression of 3:1 over RGB888 of same image.
Partial screen DYUV's can be made, but more difficult to use.
Only brightness and color differences are recorded from pixel to pixel.
