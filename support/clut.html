CD-i video formats - CLUT


Best for high contrast images.
Acceptable for natural scenes if dithering is used to accommodate 256 and fewer colors.
Has CLUT (palette).

CLUT8
256 colors - with dithering, applicable to many screens, including "natural".
Limited to a CUT transition between it and other CLUT or Run Length image types.
Full screen size of 384 x 240 -or- 384 x 280 pixels.

CLUT7
128 colors - with dithering, applicable to many screens, including "natural".
ANY video transition can be used.
Full screen size of 384 x 240 -or- 384 x 280 pixels.

CLUT4
16 colors - applicable to text or other screens with few colors.
TWICE as many pixels horizontally (across) as other image types - reduces diagonal "jaggies".
Full screen size of 768 x 240 -or- 768 x 280 pixels.
ANY video transition can be used.

Trivia
3:1 compression over RGB888.
Each pixel is independent.
Partial screen images easy to create and use.
