CD-i video formats - QHY


Good for natural images.
True High resolution of 768 x 480 -or- 768 x 560 pixels.
16.7 million colors.
Uses both still planes, limiting transition effects.
May flicker due to interlace display mode.

Trivia
Transitions involving QHY images are not as smooth.
Larger byte counts than DYUV, depending on image complexity (110k to 210k bytes).
Very complex source images may not convert (too much booster data required).
Load times from CD longer (from higher byte counts).
Some flicker noticeable in bright areas.
