CD-i video formats - Runglenght Encoding (RLE)


Best for cartoon-like images, where background and/or objects are solid colors.
Poor for complex images, like textured or dithered backgrounds and objects.
Has CLUT.

RL7
128 colors.
Visually no different than CLUT 7.
Byte count for each screen varies according to amount of horizontal detail.
Full screen size of 384 x 240 -or- 384 x 280 pixels.

RL3
8 colors.
TWICE the horizontal resolution of other formats (same as CLUT4).
Full screen size of 768 x 240 -or- 768 x 280 pixels.

Trivia
Images with large areas of solid color = low byte counts - as little a 1k byte full screen.
Low byte counts = fast loads = fast transitions.
Typical compression of 10:1 over RGB888 of same image.
