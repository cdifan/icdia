IMS Newletter

On this page, you will find several issues of the IMS Newletter, published internally by Philips Interactive Media Systems. All issues are in Portable Document Format, you need Adobe Acrobat Reader to view or print them.

Please note that none of the addresses, phone numbers and other contact information listed in these issues is valid anymore. If you have any questions about these newsletters, you may contact me. Also, please contact me if you own additional issues of IMS Newsletter either in digital form or in hard copy.
