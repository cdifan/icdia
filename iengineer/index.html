The Interactive Engineer

On this page, you will find several issues of the The Interactive Engineer newsletter that was published by Philips Media and PIMC, mainly to assist CD-i software developers. All issues are in Portable Document Format, you need Adobe Acrobat Reader to view or print them.

Please note that none of the addresses, phone numbers and other contact information listed in these issues is valid anymore. If you have any questions about these newsletters, you may contact me. Also, please contact me if you own additional copies of The Interactive Engineer either in digital form or in hard copy.
